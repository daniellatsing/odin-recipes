Chicken Fettuccine Alfredo

A restaurant classic that can be made in the comfort of your home.

A family favorite that tastes creamy but light with the inclusion of chicken, heavy cream, sauteed mushrooms, and parmesean cheese!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken breast
  • 3/4 lbs pasta
  • 1 lb (thinly sliced white mushrooms)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 1/2 cups half and half cream
  • 1/4 cup parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter

Steps:

  1. Cook pasta in a pot of salted water. Drain and set aside.
  2. Slice the chicken int ostrips and season it all over with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil over medium/high heat and saute chicken for 5 minutes. Remove and keep warm.
  4. Heat butter and saute onion for 3 minutes or until soft. Add sliced mushrooms and saute until soft (3-7 min). Add garlic and saute for 30 seconds. Stir constantly.
  5. Add half-and-half and simmer over medium/high heat for 8-10 minutes or until it begins to thicken. Add chicken, parsely, and season the sauce with salt and pepper.
  6. Stir in the pasta and warm through.
