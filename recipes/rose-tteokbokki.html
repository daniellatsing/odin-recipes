Rose Tteokbokki

The traditional Korean rice cake street food, known as Tteokbokki, with a different flavor of rosé sauce!

Rosé sauce is made from a mixture of tomato sauce and cream, creating a beautiful rose color!

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 medium onion
  • 70g mushroom
  • 2 slices bacon/ham
  • 40g tomato paste
  • 300ml water
  • 100ml heavy cream
  • 200g rice cake
  • 2 tbsp parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, thinly sliced

Steps:

  1. Pour the vegetable oil into the pan and saute the mushrooms until soft. Add in the onion, garlic, and bacon and saute onions for 3 minutes, or until soft.
  2. Add in tomato paste and mix evenly. Add in water and mix until even.
  3. Add in rice cakes and simmer until sauce thickens.
  4. Once the sauce begins to boil, add in heavy cream and mix.
  5. Season with salt, corn syrup, black pepper, and chili pepper.
  6. Mix in parmesean cheese and warm through.
