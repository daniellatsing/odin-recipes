Hong Kong Macaroni Soup

A soupy meal that can be made for any meal of the day, and warms your soul to the core!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dry elbow pasta
  • 3 cups no salt-added chicken broth
  • 1 tsp light soy sauce
  • 2 slices ham
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp chicken powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps:

  1. In a pot, pour in the chicken broth and soy sauce until boil.
  2. In another pot, boil the pasta and cook until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a pan, fry the egg until the yolk is slightly runny.
  4. Add in chicken powder and salt.
  5. Pour the cooked pasta and broth into a bowl, and top with egg and ham.
